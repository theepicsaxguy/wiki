## About goingdark.social

**goingdark.social** is a privacy-focused Mastodon instance. We're part of the Fediverse, a decentralized social network that puts users first.

### Our Values

- **Privacy First** - Your data belongs to you
- **Community Driven** - Governed by and for our members
- **Open & Transparent** - Open source and openly moderated
- **Respectful** - A safe space for meaningful conversation
