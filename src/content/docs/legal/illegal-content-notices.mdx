## DSA Point of Contact

Under the EU Digital Services Act (DSA), we provide a point of contact for reporting illegal content.

**Email:** `legal@goingdark.social`

## What Constitutes Illegal Content?

Content that violates Swedish or EU law, including but not limited to:
*   Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
*   Terrorist content
*   Incitement to violence
*   Copyright infringement (in some cases)
*   Defamation (in severe cases)

## How to Report

If you believe content on this server violates the law:
1.  Use the in-app **Report** feature if possible.
2.  For urgent legal matters, email `legal@goingdark.social` with:
    *   A link to the content
    *   An explanation of which law is being violated
    *   Your contact information (required for legal notices)

## Response Time

We aim to respond to illegal content reports within **24 hours** and take action within **48 hours** if the report is valid.

## Transparency

We publish a [Moderation Report](/docs/policies/moderation-report/) showing the number of reports received and actions taken.
