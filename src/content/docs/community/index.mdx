## How to Contribute

This community is built by its members. Here is how you can pitch in:

*   **Write Guides:** Tips for new users or self-hosting tutorials.
*   **Clarify Policy:** Help explain how [server policies](/docs/policies/) apply in real life.
*   **Documentation:** Improve our FAQ or moderation docs.
*   **Tech:** Contribute to the infrastructure code.

## Conduct

We keep this place friendly. See the [Rules](/docs/policies/rules/) for the hard boundaries, but check the [Community Guidelines](/docs/community/community-guidelines/) for the cultural expectations.
